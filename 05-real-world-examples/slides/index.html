We built one MCP App from scratch in episode four — a small dice roller. This episode is about zooming back out: what does the official example gallery look like, and who's actually shipping this in production today. Consider this the map for where to go exploring on your own after the series wraps.

The official ext-apps repository ships a genuinely wide gallery of example servers. Three-D and visualization — a CesiumJS globe, Three dot js scenes, shader effects. Data exploration tools like cohort heatmaps and customer segmentation. Business apps — scenario modelers, budget allocators. Media — PDF viewers, video, sheet music, even text-to-speech. Utilities like QR codes and system monitors. And starter templates in every major framework if you want to build your own instead of starting from scratch like we did in episode four.
