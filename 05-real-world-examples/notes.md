## Official example servers (ext-apps repo) worth a tour

- **3D / visualization**: `map-server` (CesiumJS globe), `threejs-server`,
  `shadertoy-server`
- **Data exploration**: `cohort-heatmap-server`,
  `customer-segmentation-server`, `wiki-explorer-server`
- **Business apps**: `scenario-modeler-server`, `budget-allocator-server`
- **Media**: `pdf-server`, `video-resource-server`, `sheet-music-server`,
  `say-server` (text-to-speech)
- **Utilities**: `qr-server`, `system-monitor-server`, `transcript-server`
  (speech-to-text)

All under: https://github.com/modelcontextprotocol/ext-apps/tree/main/examples
